# Privacy policy — Freestand Diagnostics

Last updated: April 2026.

## Summary

Freestand Diagnostics is built to **open and analyze backup files that you choose** on your device. It does not require an account and **does not send your training data to our servers**—there are no developer-operated backend services in this app for collecting your backup contents.

## What the app does with data

- **Import JSON**: When you use Import JSON, you select a file (for example an export from [Freestand](https://github.com/ibeshesumne/Freestand)). The app reads and parses that file **locally** to show summaries, tables, and charts.
- **In-memory / local use**: Parsed data is held in memory (and any routine local caching the system provides) for as long as you use the app. It is not uploaded by the app to a service we operate for analytics or backup inspection.
- **PDF report**: If you save a PDF report, the file is written **where you choose** (e.g. Files, iCloud Drive) using the system share/save UI. We do not receive a copy.

## Data you control

- You decide **which file** to import and **whether** to export or share a PDF.
- Uninstalling the app removes the app; **files you saved elsewhere** (exports, PDFs) remain under your control.

## Apple and system services

The app runs on Apple platforms and uses standard system frameworks (file picking, rendering, PDF generation). Apple’s handling of data is covered by [Apple’s privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. The **Last updated** date at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
